Mr. Hui (Justin), Founder / Principal / Head Tutor

Subjects I Cover: Grade 9 to 12 Mathematics (MPM1D, MPM2D, MCR3U, MHF4U, & MCV4U)

                              & Grade 11 and 12 Accounting (BAF3M & BAT4M)

About me: As the founder of Student’s First Choice Tutors Ontario, my goal is to offer educational support,

especially in mathematics and accounting, to high school students with learning disabilities, physical limitations,

and mental disorders. My program also helps students at risk of failing a course, or not motivated to attend school.

The method that makes my program very special is that I incorporate interactive learning tools such as Math BINGO,

Battleship, Kahoot, and online Jeopardy. I have many years of experience and a long history of success working with

students of diverse learning styles. Most importantly, I also ensure that the feedback I give to my students as well

as parents are always constructive and go beyond grades.

 

For students looking to excel, challenge themselves, and reach their full potential, I run a full course called

LearnInAdvance which grade 9 to 12 students in the regular mathematics stream, or AP & IB stream can enroll in to

to learn the math curriculum before taking the course. Students will become more confident in their problem solving

ability and typically attain top math marks in their school.


Interested in my services? Contact me directly at: hui@studentsfirstchoicetutors.com
